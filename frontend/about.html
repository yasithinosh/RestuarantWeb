A Family Recipe, Passed Down

La Bella Cucina was founded in 1998 when the Rossi family emigrated from Naples with two suitcases and a box of hand-written recipes from Nonna Maria. What started as a 12-seat trattoria in De Waterkant has grown into Cape Town's most beloved Italian dining destination.
